### The First Brick

HireFlow exists to study architecture through a system that can evolve under
real pressure instead of slide-deck assumptions.

- companies need to publish jobs
- candidates need to apply
- recruiters need to move people through a pipeline

> The system only becomes legible once we build it and watch the trade-offs
> appear.
